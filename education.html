ООУ Тоде Хаџи-Тефов
I started my education at my local school in my hometown, Kavadarci.
I chose this school mainly because it had great teachers, it was super close to my house and many people from my family have studied there.
I went there for 9 years, starting from 2010 to 2019.
I met most of the people I know today there and learned pretty valuable things too.
Also this is the place where I really got into computers and physics as a kid.

СОУ Гимназија Добри Даскалов
I continued my education at my local high school in Kavadarci.
There are four high schools in Kavadarci, but I chose this one because it is the most sophisticated school and has the best teacher in town.
I learned many things here and with the help of my professors, my love for computers and physics grew.
I also met some of my greatest friends here, with whom I am currently attending university.
This is the place where I made up my mind about university and what I want to do later on in my life.

Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering
After high school, I continued my education at FCSE, Skopje.
I am currently studying to get a degree for "Application of information technologies".
From the three phases of my education, I like university the most because you actually learn useful things here and also meet a lot of people from all around the country.
A major downside at FCSE is the lack of a university building (hence the question mark picture), but other than that the experience has been pretty good so far.
