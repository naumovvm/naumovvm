Welcome to my personal web-page!

I was born on September 18, 2004, in the vibrant town of Kavadarci, Macedonia.
I grew up in a loving family with my supportive parents and my older brother.
My family has always been a constant source of encouragement and inspiration throughout my life.
Throughout my life I have been interested in many things, from physics and astronomy to computers and how they work.
From a young age I was always taking apart computers and putting them back together. It was always interesting to me to see how everything worked on the hardware side. Then I started tinkering on the software side too, learning programming languages and developing little projects from time to time.
I also like to take pictures of everything, from nature to animals to urban scenery.
I have taken an interest in astrophotography, slowly gathering equipment to photograph the night sky.
I am also a gear head and a cyclist, going on solo trips to beautiful spots.
